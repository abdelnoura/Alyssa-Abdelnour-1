About Me

Alyssa Abdelnour, M.D.

I am a PGY-1 Internal Medicine Resident at the University of California Riverside. I have found a passion for providing service to resettled refugee communities, organizing educational and volunteer opportunities,  and advocating for equitable healthcare. 


In my free time, I enjoy creating artwork with mediums such as: graphic design, acrylic and watercolor paint, and oil pastels.
